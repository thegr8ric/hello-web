Case Study

Bloom Coffee Co.

A full identity refresh for a specialty coffee startup preparing to launch subscriptions and retail shelves.

Project Breakdown

We rebuilt the visual identity around warmth, clarity, and consistency. Deliverables included logo lockups, packaging direction, social templates, and launch art direction for paid campaigns.

Challenge

The previous look felt generic and inconsistent across packaging and digital touchpoints, reducing trust during the brand launch phase.

Solution

A modular identity system with clear hierarchy, flexible layouts, and reusable social components that kept every channel aligned.

Outcome

Stronger visual recognition, faster campaign production, and a polished brand rollout that felt premium from day one.

Design Info

Brand Strategy

Positioning centered on warmth, craft quality, and everyday clarity. We defined messaging pillars and visual cues for packaging, web, and social.
