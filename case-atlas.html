Launch visuals for a boutique fitness studio focused on high-energy classes and modern coaching programs.

Project Breakdown

The project combined campaign art direction with conversion-focused web sections. We created reusable launch assets for ads, social posts, landing pages, and in-studio materials.

Challenge

The studio needed a bold launch identity that looked premium while still feeling approachable for new members.

Solution

We built a high-contrast visual style, organized typography hierarchy, and a kit of campaign layouts for different offer periods.

Outcome

The launch achieved strong local awareness and gave the team a repeatable design system for monthly promotions.
