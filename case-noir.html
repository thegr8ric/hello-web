Case Study

Noir Skin Lab

Packaging and campaign direction for a skincare brand releasing seasonal product drops and digital promos.

Back to Main Page Request Similar Project

Project Breakdown

This case focused on premium positioning through packaging hierarchy, campaign consistency, and quick-turn social ad adaptations for product drops.

Challenge

Each product drop looked visually disconnected, making the brand feel inconsistent and harder to recognize at a glance.

Solution

We defined a system for typography, color accents, and packaging structures that scaled across new launches without redesigning from scratch.

Outcome

The brand gained a cohesive premium look and a faster workflow for both packaging updates and promotional campaign assets.

Design Info

Packaging System

Consistent label architecture and typographic hierarchy designed for quick drop-to-drop updates while preserving premium brand recognition.
